Construction Process & Timeline

Understanding our construction process and project timelines

How much time does completion of a project take?

+

The project completion depends on the capacity and complexity of the project. However, we offer a proper plan and timeline for individual projects accordingly:

  • Small residential projects: 3-6 months
  • Medium homes/commercial: 6-12 months
  • Large commercial/industrial: 12-24 months
  • Renovation projects: 1-3 months

We provide detailed schedules with milestone dates during the planning phase.

What is included in your free estimate?

+

Our comprehensive free estimate includes:

  • Site visit and assessment
  • Detailed material breakdown and costs
  • Labor costs and timeline
  • Project phases and milestones
  • Equipment and machinery requirements
  • Permits and regulatory compliance costs
  • Payment schedule recommendations

We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

+

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and approvals including:

  • Building permits from local authorities
  • Environmental clearances when required
  • Safety and fire department approvals
  • Utility connections (electricity, gas, water)
  • Compliance with local building codes

Our team is familiar with Lahore regulations and ensures full compliance.

Cost & Payment

Understanding pricing, payment structures, and budget planning

Does the construction company show concern regarding budget?

+

Absolutely! We understand the range and budget of our clients. We try to plan construction packages that are customer-friendly and reasonable. We manage to make plans according to clients' budgets:

  • Budget-conscious material selection
  • Phased construction options
  • Value engineering to optimize costs
  • Transparent cost breakdowns
  • No surprise charges or hidden fees

How are payments structured?

+

We use milestone-based payment structure for transparency and client protection:

  • Contract signing: 10-15% advance
  • Foundation completion: 25-30%
  • Structure completion: 30-35%
  • Finishing work: 20-25%
  • Final handover: 5-10%

Payment schedule is detailed in the contract and tied to completed work stages.

What factors affect construction costs?

+

Several factors influence the total construction cost:

  • Project size and complexity
  • Quality of materials chosen
  • Site conditions and accessibility
  • Design complexity and customizations
  • Current market rates for materials
  • Timeline and urgency requirements
  • Special features or technologies

We provide detailed cost breakdowns so you understand exactly what drives the pricing.

Getting Started

How to begin your construction project with us

How do I get started with my construction project?

+

Getting started is simple with our streamlined process:

  1. Initial Contact: Call us or fill our contact form
  2. Consultation: Free discussion about your requirements
  3. Site Visit: We visit your location for assessment
  4. Proposal: Detailed estimate and timeline within 48 hours
  5. Planning: Design development and permit applications
  6. Construction: Project execution with regular updates

We guide you through every step of the process.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

+

To help us provide the most accurate estimate, please prepare:

  • Project location and site details
  • Approximate project size and scope
  • Your budget range
  • Timeline expectations
  • Any specific requirements or preferences
  • Existing plans or sketches (if available)
  • Property documents (for new construction)

Don't worry if you don't have all details - we'll help you develop the complete project scope.
